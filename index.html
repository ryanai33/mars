Acreage & Semi-Rural · Property ID 752

Set on nearly 1.5 hectares of gently contoured semi-rural land in sought-after Okura, this substantial masonry residence delivers 443m² of considered living across six bedrooms arranged in two independent wings — purpose-built for multi-generational living. Three separate living areas, four bathrooms, a generous butler's pantry and seven garaging spaces combine to create a lifestyle of genuine ease, privacy and flexibility. Landscaped grounds, a peaceful pond and proximity to a nearby boat ramp and marine reserve complete an exceptionally rare offering.
